Tiers

How decisions flow.

Political tier

The Honourable Minister and the Minister of State are the political leadership, accountable to the President and the National Assembly. They direct policy, defend the Ministry's budget and represent the Government on defence matters.

Administrative tier

The Permanent Secretary heads the Civil Service side of the Ministry and is the accounting officer. The PS provides continuity across political cycles, leads departmental directors and chairs the Ministerial Tenders Board.

Operational tier

The Defence Headquarters, under the Chief of Defence Staff, exercises joint operational command over the Nigerian Army, Nigerian Navy and Nigerian Air Force — each headed by its own Chief of Service.

Parastatal tier

Agencies and parastatals — NDA, NDC, AFCSC, DICON, NAFRC, MPB, DIA — operate under MOD oversight with statutory mandates ranging from training to industrial production to welfare administration.
