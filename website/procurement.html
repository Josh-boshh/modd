How to Bid

Bidder guide.

Step 01

Register on NOCOPO

Register with the Nigerian Open Contracting Portal (NOCOPO) and on the BPP database of contractors.

Step 02

Obtain bidding documents

Download standard bidding documents from this page or collect printed copies at the Department of Procurement, Ship House.

Step 03

Submit before deadline

Submit sealed bids before the closing date stated on the notice. Late submissions are rejected.
