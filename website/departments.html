Components of MOD

Civilian departments.

Sixteen departments make up the civilian side of the Federal Ministry of Defence — each led by a Director reporting to the Permanent Secretary, supporting the political leadership in driving the Ministry's mandate.

01

Army Affairs

Coordinates policy, finance and welfare matters between the Ministry and the Nigerian Army. Director: Mr. SM Attah.
02

Navy Affairs

Coordinates policy, finance and welfare matters between the Ministry and the Nigerian Navy. Director: Mr. Joel Adeoye Christopher.
03

Air Force Affairs

Coordinates policy, finance and welfare matters between the Ministry and the Nigerian Air Force. Director: Dr. Ahmad Ibrahim Sulaiman.
04

Joint Services

Coordinates tri-service operations, joint task forces and combined exercises. Director: Mrs. B.O. Olaniyi.
05

Human Resource Management

Recruitment, promotion, discipline, training, welfare and pensions for Ministry civilian staff. Director: Mal. Idris Shehu Gaya, mni.
06

Finance and Accounts

Budget preparation, payments, financial reporting and treasury management. Director: Mr. Afolabi Idowu Omoniwa.
07

Procurement

Open, competitive tendering in line with the Public Procurement Act 2007. Director: Mr. Otalike P. Yahaya.
08

Planning, Research & Statistics

Defence policy research, strategic planning, monitoring and evaluation. Director: Alh. Abdulrahman Suleiman.
09

Legal Services

Legal advisory, contracts, treaties and dispute resolution. Director: Barr. Adebola Odugbesa.
10

Health Services

Military healthcare policy, medical reciprocity and the Ministry's medical interventions. Director: Dr. Jibrin Alhassan.
11

General Services

Estate, facilities, transport, security and protocol for the Ministry HQ. Director: Uwaneyi Raymond Erurane.
12

Information, Press & Public Relations

Public communications, press releases, media accreditation and citizen engagement. Director: Mr. Henshaw Ogubike.
13

Education Services

Liaison with the NDA, AFCSC, NDC and other Defence training institutions. Director: Mr. Kura Markus.
14

Internal Audit

Independent assurance over financial controls and value for money. Director: Mrs. Gloria Okopi.
15

Reform Coordination & Service Improvement

Implementation of public service reform and SERVICOM commitments. Director: Nneji Nkiru Florence.
16

Office of the Permanent Secretary

Direct administrative support to the Permanent Secretary; secretariat of the Ministry's senior management.
