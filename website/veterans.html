For Citizens

Veterans of the Armed Forces.

Honouring those who have served. The Federal Ministry of Defence coordinates pensions, resettlement, healthcare and welfare programmes for retired personnel of the Nigerian Army, Navy and Air Force, and their families.

Resettlement

NAFRC — Skills & business training

The Nigerian Armed Forces Resettlement Centre (NAFRC), Oshodi, retrains retiring and retired personnel in trades, agriculture, business and professional disciplines. Courses run on a rolling intake.

About NAFRC
Pensions

Military Pensions Board (MPB)

MPB administers pensions, gratuities and survivors' benefits for retired Armed Forces personnel and their dependants, in line with the Pensions Reform Act and applicable defence rules.

About MPB
Healthcare

Military hospitals & medical reciprocity

Veterans have access to military hospitals nationwide and to civilian referral arrangements under the medical reciprocity framework coordinated by the Department of Health Services.

Housing

Post-service housing

The Ministry works with the Federal Mortgage Bank, the Armed Forces Housing Schemes and the FCDA on dedicated post-service housing for retirees.
