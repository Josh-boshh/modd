Honourable Minister of State for Defence

Dr. Bello Mohammed Matawalle

Former Governor of Zamfara State and Member of the Order of the Niger (MON), Dr. Matawalle supports the Honourable Minister on strategic, security-sector and welfare portfolios, with particular focus on community-level peace-building, banditry response and counter-violent-extremism programmes.

His extensive experience as a state Chief Executive in one of Nigeria's most security-challenged regions equips the Ministry with on-the-ground perspective and political reach into the North-West and frontier communities.
