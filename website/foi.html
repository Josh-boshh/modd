Freedom of Information.

The Federal Ministry of Defence is bound by the Freedom of Information Act, 2011. Citizens, journalists and organisations have the right to request any record held by the Ministry, subject only to the narrow statutory exemptions.

Submit a request

FOI request form.

Complete the form below to submit your request. The Ministry will respond within 7 working days as required by the FOI Act.

Be as specific as possible — this helps the responsible officer locate the records quickly.
Information

How it works.

  1. Submit: use the form (left) or send a letter to the FOI Desk Officer at Ship House.
  2. Acknowledgement: we acknowledge in writing within 3 working days with a reference number.
  3. Response: records or a written reasoned reply within 7 working days of receipt.
  4. Appeal: if you disagree with the response you may appeal to the Federal High Court under §20 of the FOI Act.
Exemptions. The Ministry can withhold a record only if its disclosure would damage national defence, foreign relations, an ongoing investigation, or fall within another narrow statutory exemption (FOI Act §11–17). Where exemptions apply we will tell you so and cite the section.

Annual FOI report

Each year the Ministry publishes the number of FOI requests received, granted, partially granted and refused. The latest report is available on request.
